ENBA PhD Call Webinar

IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca (Italy) organizes a webinar series to inform potentially interested students on the PhD program in Economics, Networks and Business Analytics.

Selected members of the IMT faculty will present the structure and activity of ENBA (Economics, Networks and Business Analytics). Prof. Ennio Bilancini will introduce the research topics in economics and behavioral sciences, Prof. Guido Caldarelli will focus on network science. Then, Alessia Patuelli will shortly describe business studies at IMT. To conclude, Falco J. Bargagli Stoffi will bring a student point of view describing IMT activities in the field of machine learning and data analytics.
